Warranty Information

1. What does the warranty cover?

Our warranty covers manufacturing defects, engine performance, and transmission issues for all vehicles purchased from our dealership.

2. How long is the warranty period?

The warranty period is 3 years or 60,000 kilometers, whichever comes first. Extended warranty options are also available.

3. Are there any exclusions?

The warranty does not cover wear-and-tear items such as tires, brake pads, and wiper blades. Damage caused by accidents, misuse, or unauthorized modifications is also excluded.

4. How can I claim my warranty?

To claim your warranty, please visit our dealership with your warranty card and proof of purchase. Our service team will assist you with the process.

5. Can I transfer the warranty to a new owner?

Yes, the warranty is transferable to a new owner if the vehicle is sold during the warranty period. Please contact us for more details.
